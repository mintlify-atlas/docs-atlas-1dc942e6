Betterflow is a free, fully browser-based screenshot editor that lets you transform plain screenshots into polished, professional visuals — without installing anything, creating an account, or dealing with watermarks. Whether you need a striking product shot, an animated demo, or a multi-slide presentation, Betterflow handles it all directly in your browser using a powerful canvas engine built on Next.js, React 19, and Konva.

## How It Works

Betterflow follows a straightforward three-step workflow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your screenshot">
    Drag and drop an image onto the canvas, use the file picker, or capture a website screenshot directly from the editor. The Chrome extension also lets you record your screen and send the recording straight to the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Style and animate">
    Pick a background, add device frames or borders, apply 3D perspective transforms, drop in text or image overlays, and optionally attach an animation preset from the timeline panel. Every control updates the canvas in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download a high-resolution PNG or JPG (up to 5× scale) or export your animation as MP4, WebM, or GIF — all processed entirely in the browser without any server round-trips.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Free, Open Source, No Watermarks

Betterflow is released under the **Apache License 2.0** and is completely free to use. There is no account to create, no subscription to manage, and no watermark added to your exports. The live version is available at [betterflow.site](https://betterflow.site), and the full source code is on GitHub.
